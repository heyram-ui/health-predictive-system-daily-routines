WHO Health Standards

Official guidelines from the World Health Organization for a healthy life.

Physical Activity

Adults (18-64 years):

  • Should do at least 150–300 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic physical activity.
  • Or at least 75–150 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic physical activity.
  • Muscle-strengthening activities at moderate or greater intensity that involve all major muscle groups on 2 or more days a week.

Healthy Diet

Key facts:

  • Fruit, vegetables, legumes (e.g. lentils and beans), nuts and whole grains (e.g. unprocessed maize, millet, oats, wheat and brown rice).
  • At least 400 g (i.e. five portions) of fruit and vegetables per day.
  • Less than 10% of total energy intake from free sugars.
  • Less than 30% of total energy intake from fats.
  • Less than 5 g of salt (equivalent to about one teaspoon) per day.

Sleep Hygiene

Recommendations:

  • 7-9 hours of good quality sleep on a regular schedule.
  • Avoid caffeine and heavy meals close to bedtime.
  • Limit exposure to bright light (screens) in the evenings.
